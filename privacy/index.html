Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2025

At GEODHA, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the GEODHA mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: To allow users to report issues accurately, we collect approximate location information (only with your permission).
  • User Reports: We collect data on community issues submitted by users, including categories like littering, water scarcity, and power outages.
  • Device Information: We may collect anonymous data such as device type, OS version, and crash logs to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display reported issues on public heatmaps.
  • To improve app features and user experience.
  • To generate anonymized insights for public awareness and research.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share anonymized data with research institutions or local authorities for public interest purposes.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data using secure servers, encryption, and access controls. However, no method of transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

  • You may disable location permissions at any time via your device settings.
  • You may request data deletion by contacting us at contact@geodha.org.
